Campus Recruitment Training

Skillbit HR SERVICES is a professionally managed and a rapidly expanding recruitment and staffing a firm specialized in the field of Campus Recruitment Training Programs.

With over experience with student in recruitment industry, we at SKILLBIT HR SERVICES realized the need of such quality training program for students to improve their employability skills, as it is the, demand from the corporate world. We take pride in introducing ourselves as one of the fast growing and the most trusted business partner of some very affluent corporate companies.

The main objective of our CRT program is Skill enhancement by bridging the gap between Academic and the Industry by taking inputs from the industry experts and train students on various skill sets that will help them get adequately skilled, and more readily employable. Our main focus is on complete transformation of student into working professional. Though available in abundance, jobs do not come easy. One needs to have the right mix of academic, technical, behavioural and professional skills to be reckoned as a deserving candidate. Campus Recruitment Training Program focuses on developing the student’s overall soft skills, personality and other professional skills, making them more eligible for jobs.
