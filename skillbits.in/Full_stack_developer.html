FULL STACK DEVELOPER

This is an excellent opportunity for an entry level Java Developer to join a veteran team whose mission is to deliver a commercial software product. The American Bureau of Shipping and its Nautical Systems Division is not your typical large service provider or outsourcer. If you are looking for something different and want direct responsibility for a commercial product, not just a project, we want to talk to you.

SkillBit is one of the leading providers of integrated asset management software covering principal aspects of vessel operations such as Regulatory Requirements, Crew and Payroll Management, Purchasing and Planned Maintenance, Energy Management and Vetting. It is an ERP-like system utilized by a unique market of some of the world’s largest vessel, boat and offshorerig operators.

Required Skills

2-5 years of experience in the following:

• Expert in HTML5, JavaScript and CSS

• In-depth of knowledge of frameworks like r, jQuery

• UI toolkits like KendoUI, Infragistics, jQueryUI

• Coding in Core JAVA/J2EE, Spring, JDBC

• MS- SQL, My-SQL, Database Knowledge

• Object Oriented Programming (OOP) concepts

2-3 years of experience in the following:

• OOD, UML Design

• Tomcat, SVN – open source technologies

Highly Desired Skills:

• Jasper Reports, iReport

• SOA, SOAP, XML, XSL, Webservices

• Installer scripting using Nullsoft

The Java Developer/Designer will be expected to take on responsibility for design, development and code quality. The ideal candidate for this role will have:

• Working experience in all aspects of web development from high level business requirements

• Produce high quality modern user experience supporting various devices from smartphones to desktops

• A provable track record of experience using MSSQL Server and MySQL

• Strong SQL query writing and testing skills

• Good understanding of Service Oriented Architectures, Web Services Component Architectures

• Very solid coding skills used in individual contribution and code reviews of other team members

• The ability to troubleshoot issues quickly in support of hotfix demands as well as operate against a planned development schedule

Must have good communication skills, an appreciation for systems lifecycle methodologies and a commitment to process adherence for you will be asked to:

• Manage your work to agreed upon goals and objectives, including schedules, milestones and cost

• Contribute to process improvement initiatives and lead by example to meet schedule and scope commitments

• Collaborate with quality assurance team members to structure test scenarios and plans

• Operate in an agile scrum style with a mindset toward creating workable interim deliverables that can be demonstrated to clients and product managers

DETAILS:

Experience: 2-5 years
Angular JS, Java, Spring MVC, REST, Tomcat
Job Location: Pune,India
